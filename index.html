V2

The V2 was a weapon designed near the end of World War II to try to win the war for Germany

  • The V2 was called the Retribution Weapon 2 as the first (V1) was shot down.
  • To avoid being shot down it was launched into space.
  • It was first used on the 20th of June 1944.
